About Me

Welcome to my journey of discovering and documenting the fascinating world of the Spiny Babbler, Nepal's endemic bird species. With over 15 years of experience in wildlife research and conservation, I have dedicated my life to studying these remarkable creatures.

My Mission

My mission is to raise awareness about the Spiny Babbler and contribute to its conservation through research, documentation, and education. I believe that understanding and appreciating these unique birds is crucial for their survival.

Experience & Expertise

  • 15+ years of field research experience
  • Published 25+ research papers on Nepalese avifauna
  • Wildlife photography award winner
  • Active member of Bird Conservation Nepal
  • Lead researcher in Spiny Babbler habitat studies
